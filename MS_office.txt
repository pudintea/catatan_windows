Masalah :
this product can't be installed on the selected update channel

Solusi :
1. Open registry editor.
2. Go through this path: Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Office\16.0\Common\OfficeUpdate
3. Delete OfficeUpdate key
4. Restart your computer (recommended)
5. Install your Microsoft 365!


Solusi 2 :
1. Open registry editor.
2. Go through this path: Computer\HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Office\ClickToRun
3. Delete OfficeUpdate key
4. Restart your computer (recommended)
5. Install your Microsoft 365!
